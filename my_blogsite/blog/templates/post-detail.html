Moreover, it also makes us fitter both mentally and physically. It can be easily that those who have tried it have said to come out as better people through this experience.

Above all, people also take up mountain climbing to set new records or break the older ones. But, it is safe to say that most of them do it for the thrill of the experience and doing something adventurous.
